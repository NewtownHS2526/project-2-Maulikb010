Facts about Lions

some things to know

Some facts

Primary Hunters

Lionesses are the main hunters in the pride, using teamwork and agility to take down prey.

Male Privilege

Despite the lionesses' hunting efforts, males eat first.

Prey

Their diet includes large animals like wildebeest, buffalo, and zebra, but they will also eat smaller animals like birds and hares.

Scavenging

Lions will also scavenge for food, and they can go up to four days without water but must eat daily to survive.
