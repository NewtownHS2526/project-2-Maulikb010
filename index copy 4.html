Conservation Status and Threats

The problems

Issues

  • Lions are considered vulnerable, which means they are at risk of becoming endangered. Their numbers are going down because of several problems.
  • Their homes are getting smaller because people are building farms and cities. Lions sometimes get into conflicts with humans, and some are killed to protect livestock..
  • Lions are hunted for their bones, skin, claws, and other body parts, which are used in traditional medicine, souvenirs, and jewelry. This illegal hunting, known as poaching, greatly harms lion populations and threatens their survival in the wild.
  • Many groups are working to protect lions by creating wildlife parks and making laws against hunting. We need to keep working to help lions stay safe and live in the wild.
