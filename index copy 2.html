Lions social structure

The pride

What is a pride

  • Pride is a group of lions which typically includes related females, their cubs, and a few adult males

Roles in pride

  • The roles in the pride is that males defend the pride while females hunt and raising cubs

Communication in pride

  • The prides use roaring to communicate over long distances, mark territory, and find each other.
